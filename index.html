Road Trip to Niagara Falls, 2015

Picture it: St. Louis, 2015.
A family with passports ready to expire.
A car with enough space for all of them (and acceptable gas mileage).
This and several late nights on Google Maps lead them to plan one excellent road trip to Niagra Falls, Canada.

The route included:

  • Regent Theater Mural
  • Hartman Rock Garden
  • Urban Handmade & Yellow Springs, OH
  • Niagara, CA!
  • The Falls & Hornblower Boat Ride

From St. Louis, MO to Springfield, OH

Regent Theater Mural

This mural is a tribute to Gustave (Gus) Ferdinand Sun, who rose to fame as the premier small town vaudeville king in the early 1900s. It's painted on the Regent Theater, which was opened in 1920. Mr. Ferdinand and the theater both have an interesting history.
